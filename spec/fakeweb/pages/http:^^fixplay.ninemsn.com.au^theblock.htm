The Block

Australia | Reality
The Block is back, bigger, harder and more competitive than ever — because this time each team will renovate not an apartment but an entire house. Four couples are taken out of their comfort zones and dropped into a frantic contest to renovate equally dilapidated houses in the inner Melbourne suburb of Richmond.
