Jeeves and Wooster

Comedy drama series following the insane shenanigans of Bertram Wooster (Hugh Laurie) and his faithful butler Jeeves (Stephen Fry). Each episode sees Wooster unwittingly caught up in some kind of scrape, and each time it's down to his trusty aide Jeeves to come up with a cunning masterplan to get him off the hook.
