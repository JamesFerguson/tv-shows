Gossip Girl

USA | Drama
Gossip Girl is an American teen drama series based on the book series written by Cecily von Ziegesar. Narrated by the omniscient yet unseen blogger "Gossip Girl", voiced by Kristen Bell, the series revolves around the lives of privileged teenagers attending an elite private school on Manhattan's Upper East Side in New York City.
