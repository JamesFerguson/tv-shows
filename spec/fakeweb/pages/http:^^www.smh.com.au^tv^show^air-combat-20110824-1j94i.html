Air Combat

Channel: Military

Air Combat covers the history of military aviation from World War I to the present day, focusing on famous aerial engagements, heroic pilots and a dramatic array of aircraft from Harrier Jump Jets to B-52 Bombers. History comes alive through the feats of the men whose heroics in the air changed the shape of war, and whose courage lives in legend.
