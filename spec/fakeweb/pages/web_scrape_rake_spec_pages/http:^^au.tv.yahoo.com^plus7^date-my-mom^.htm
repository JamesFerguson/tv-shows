    Date My Mom

    Reality series in which an attractive young guy agrees to be fixed up on three dates in search of love, but instead of dating three girls, the tables are turned and he finds himself dating the girls' mothers. The guy will then select the daughter he wishes to go out with based on his date with her mother, and each participant will be rewarded with a small cash prize.
