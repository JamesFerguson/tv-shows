Rescue

Australia | Drama
They are the elite of the elite. Heroes who risk their lives to rescue others. They are the men and women of Rescue Special Ops (RSO). Experienced paramedics in peak physical condition, they're skilled in abseiling, rock climbing, surf and sea rescue, 4wd driving, and fire brigade procedures. They're the first to be called in for any natural or man-made disaster, salvage or rescue operations.
