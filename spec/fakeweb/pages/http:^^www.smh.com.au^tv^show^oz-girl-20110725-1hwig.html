Oz Girl

Oz Girl

Channel: Drama

Australia’s 23-part award-winning web series OzGirl tells the story of Sadie Brown, played by Neighbours star Sophie Tilson, a country girl who moves to the city to stay with her former child-star cousin, Megan Brown, played by Shanrah Wakefield.

OzGirl was created by Melbourne-based producer Nicholas Carlton and won Best Web Series and Best Acting at Independent Television Festival in Los Angeles, was nominated for best drama at The Streamy Awards, had a special screening at the New York TV Festival and was an official honouree at the Webby Awards.
