Style By Jury

View before and after photos»

Makeover series that focuses on making the best first impression possible, through dramatic makeovers. Over the course of seven days, makeover candidates get transformed from the inside out. With the help of experts in the field of cosmetic dermatology, cosmetic dentistry, image, fashion, hair and make-up, they show how one can look 10 years younger and get that "wow" factor.

Classified: PG Parental Guidance is recommended

Genre: Reality/Real Life
