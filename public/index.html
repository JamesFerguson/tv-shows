What is this?

This site provides a directory of all TV shows and episodes freely available online from Australian providers. It lets you subscribe to individual shows so you can see quickly which of the shows you watch have new episodes available when you want to watch something.

Why use this?

If you watch online TV a fair bit you probably spend a lot of time just trying to find out which of the shows you like has a new episode available.

How does it work?

This problem - wanting to be able to know at a glance if a site has new content you care about - has existed for a long time on the internet, and it has already been solved.

There are a bunch of apps - web-based and desktop-based - called Feed Readers that allow you to subscribe to supporting websites that frequently have new content, like news sites.

If you noticed the word ’supporting’ in the last sentence, that’s basically why this site exists - it seems only the ABC supports subscribing to a list of its online shows, and it does so in a fairly braindead fashion.
