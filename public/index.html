Why use this site?

If you watch online TV a fair bit you probably spend a lot of time just trying to find out which of the shows you like has a new episode available. Using the feeds this site provides, you’ll always be able to tell that at a glance.

What is this?

This site provides a feed of all TV shows freely available to watch online from Australian providers (except SBS and a few shows from Ten at the moment). Each TV show item in the feed provides a link to another feed of all of its available episodes.

How does I use it?

Simple. Subscribe to the All Shows feed to get a list of all the shows out there. Then subscribe to the shows you like. Your feed reader will tell you how many new episodes are in the feed (i.e. available now online) and regularly check back with the feed to keep that count up to date.

No more trawling through TV station’s sites for ages only to find there’s nothing you want to watch!

What’s a feed reader? There are a bunch of apps - web-based and desktop-based, many free to use - that allow you to subscribe to supporting websites that frequently have new content, like news sites. For example Google Reader and Feed.ly are free web based feed readers, NetNewsWire is an ad-supported desktop feed reader for the Mac, and I’m sure there are heaps of desktop readers for windows.
